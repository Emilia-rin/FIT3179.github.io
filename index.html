World Happiness Report

This visualisation is about the state of happiness of people all over the world from 2015 till 2021.

Figure 1

Total Score of Economy, Family, Health, Freedom, Trust and Generosity

Figure 2

Happiness Score and Economy of different Regions

From the distribution of the points on the scatter plot, it is easily shown that the Sub-Saharan Africa is more likely has lower economy score which results lower happiness score. On the other hand, the countries of Western Europe is more likely to have higher happiness score with high economy score.

Figure 3

Economy of Countries

The heatmap shows the Economy of the countries of different regions. Compare the happiness score with the color of economy shown on the heatmap, most countries with higher happiness score also has high economy score.
